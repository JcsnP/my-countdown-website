Privacy Policy for My Countdown


Thank you for using My Countdown. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.


1. Information We Collect

We collect limited data through third-party services to improve the app and its functionality:

  • RevenueCat: Used for managing in-app purchases and subscriptions. It may collect transaction information but not personal data like names or emails.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Used to track app performance and crash reports. This service collects anonymized data related to app usage and errors to help us fix issues and improve the app.

No personal data such as names, email addresses, or event details are collected by our app.


2. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction, or deletion of any data related to your app usage stored by third-party services. Since the app itself does not collect personal information, all events and data you add to the app are stored locally on your device. You may delete or modify this data at any time through the app's settings.


3. Data Security

We prioritize your data security. The app uses industry-standard security practices to ensure the limited data collected by third-party services is protected.
